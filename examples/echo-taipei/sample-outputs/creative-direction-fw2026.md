## Direction A — 城市侵蝕 (Urban Corrosion)

**Concept:** Material degradation as identity. Taipei's concrete infrastructure — stained overpasses, oxidized railings, rain-darkened stone — rendered as a luxury aesthetic.

**Aesthetic Pillars:** Corroded / Structural / Permanent

**Color Story:**
- Primary: Tungsten `#2C2C2E` (reinforced — brand core)
- Secondary: Oxidized Copper `#7A7060`
- Accent: Rust Bleed `#8B4513`
- Surface: Wet Concrete `#6B6B6D`
- Contrast: Stark White `#F8F8F8` (minimal use, maximum impact)

**Typography Feel:** Extended grotesque, condensed headline weight. Industrial. No serifs.

**Texture / Material Language:** Boiled wool, washed canvas, recycled nylon with visible weave, unlined seams.

**Key Reference Universe:**
- Craig Green AW2020 (structural decomposition)
- Yohji Yamamoto — specifically the worn, lived-in quality, not the theatricality
- Wu Tsang's film work — the way Taipei infrastructure becomes abstraction

**Motion Signature:** Slow. Wind-affected fabric movement. Nothing artificially fast.

**What We Reject:** Fashion week "dystopia" aesthetics (already exhausted), artificially distressed for its own sake, post-apocalyptic signaling.

**Recommendation:** ✅ Direction A — most aligned with brand DNA. The Taipei-specificity of the reference universe is impossible to fake.

## Direction B — 深夜殘餘 (Late Night Remainder)

**Concept:** The city at 3am — not dangerous, not glamorous, just real. The people still there: kitchen staff, venue sound engineers, the last table at a 24-hour noodle shop.

**Aesthetic Pillars:** Nocturnal / Functional / Witnessed

**Color Story:**
- Primary: Near-Black `#1A1A1C`
- Secondary: Sodium Lamp `#D4A847` (warm amber from streetlight)
- Accent: Cold Fluorescent `#B8C8D0`
- Surface: Matte Textile `#2E2E30`

**Typography Feel:** Monospaced secondary type (receipt/timestamp quality), bold grotesque primary.

**Texture / Material Language:** Heavy jersey, ripstop, thermal-quilted liners, cotton fleece with texture face.

**Key Reference Universe:**
- Daido Moriyama's late-night Shinjuku photography (adapted to Taipei)
- NEIGHBORHOOD's workwear utility applied to nightlife
- Edward Yang films — not the plots, the ambient light and space between people

**Motion Signature:** Static. People in stillness, environment in motion (cars passing, light flickering).

**What We Reject:** "Night market" as aesthetic shorthand, neon, Gen Z Y2K nostalgia, over-lit editorial glamour.

## Direction C — 極簡建檔 (Minimal Archive)

**Concept:** A brand archiving itself. Not nostalgia — documentation. Every piece is a record.

**Aesthetic Pillars:** Archival / Considered / Quiet

**Color Story:**
- Primary: Aged Paper `#D4C9B0`
- Secondary: Iron Oxide `#4A3728`
- Accent: Deep Navy `#1C2B3A`
- Surface: Off-White `#F2EDE4`

*(Note: lighter base — significant departure from current brand dark base. Higher risk / higher contrast with brand's existing identity.)*

**Typography Feel:** Light-weight grotesque, generous tracking. Looks like specimen sheets or technical documentation.

**Motion Signature:** Still. Not even wind. Museum quality.

**What We Reject:** Nostalgic sepia, vintage-for-vintage's-sake, "heritage" as a costume.

## FW2026 Visual Direction Doc

**Season spirit (one paragraph):**

FW2026 for ECHO TAIPEI is the city at the moment just before weather. The hour when Taipei's concrete absorbs moisture and the air carries rust and old oil. Not beautiful in the conventional sense. Precisely right in the way that only specific places are specific. The collection is about permanence through material — things that absorb time rather than resist it.

**Campaign photography brief:**
- Locations: Level B2 of a Da'an parking structure (2am), the alley behind a venue on Zhongshan N. Rd., the underside of an overpass near Minsheng Community
- Light: Sodium vapor (amber-yellow) mixed with cold fluorescent spill. No golden hour.
- People: Bo-Yan types. Doing nothing conspicuous.
- Post: Slightly underexposed. Pull shadows toward cool blue. Highlights slightly blown but not clipped.
- Avoid: Tripod-clean compositions. Everything should look like it could have been a still from a 16mm film nobody saw.
